Seed  Pulverizing

 

 

The last step is the pulverizing or the grinding process. In this step after pounding the seeds you now need to make it into a powder by using a blender with grinder or anything that can make the seed into powder form. To refine the seed you can use a strainer to remove big particles from the powder. After that you now have a chocolate powder from the Jackfruit seeds. You can make it into chocolate candies or make it into a hot chocolate drink.
