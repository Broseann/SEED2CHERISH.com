About Us 

 

Bañas Seed to Cherish is a company website encouraging people nowadays to make a valuable product out of seeds. Jackfruit Seeds are usually thrown away so instead of throwing, we think of a way to make it into a chocolate powder as a substitute to the commercialized cocoa powder. Our basis of making this powder is a research study conducted by the students of Ragay Science and Math Oriented High School in the year 2018.
