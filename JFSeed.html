Jackfruit Seed

 

 

The Jackfruit Seed is a very familiar seed for us. We usually throw it in the trashcan after eating the delicious meet of the Jackfruit. Some of us have the idea of cooking the seed through boiling and adding a small amount of salt. Other people, like a farmer for example would collect the seed and plant for the next generation of Jackfruit tree. But most of us usually does not give enough value to these seeds and just throw it away and let the nature handle everything. Two things can happen it can grow or it will rot along with other biodegradable materials in the ground.
